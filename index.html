Our story

Three generations of recipes, one open kitchen

Zaiqa House began as a family Sunday tradition: charcoal lit at noon, karahi served at dusk. In 2019 we opened our doors so the rest of Hyderabad could pull up a chair.

Everything is made in-house daily: the masalas are ground each morning, the naan dough proofs overnight, and the BBQ never touches a gas flame. You can watch it all happen — the kitchen has no walls.
